8/26/23

  1. Big balance changes:
    1. Deflated everything to about 1/3 value to make prices closer, then did the following:
    2. Made upgrades more expensive
    3. Nerfed long growth high value crops (cauliflower, pumpkin, melon) due to their abnormally high profit/min
    4. Anyone with over $100,000 will have their $ lowered to $100,000 due to the old system being too inflated and to not create old now unbreakable records
  2. Fertilizer:
    1. Unlock fertilizer as reward in order board
    2. Put fertilizer on crop tiles to give them temporary bonuses
    3. Growth time fertilizer (blue): Crops grow 2x as fast for 10 minutes
    4. Harvests fertilizer (Pink): Next 5 planted seeds receive one extra harvest
    5. Yields fertilizer (Yellow): Next 10 harvests receive one bonus crops (1-3 depending on crop)
  3. Unlocks now operate on levels instead of straight XP
  4. Increased level needed to unlock certain goods (now takes longer to unlock evreything)
    1. Anyone with over 25,000 XP (highest unlock) will have their XP lowered to 25,000 due to the old system being too inflated and to not create old now unbreakable records
  5. A few friendly GUI changes
  6. Added 'add all' buttons to artisan machines
